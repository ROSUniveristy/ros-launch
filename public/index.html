The skill fidelity from a collage academia to that of an industrial professional
is an important metric for any growing economy.

The Rosversity initiative is an attempt to increase this metric.

Our mission is to develop a culture of collaboration between innovative thinkers in both the industrial and academia to provide R&D as a service to our society.

We are an online robotics education platform providing the students an oppertunity to be part of industry grade hands on projects even before graduation.
